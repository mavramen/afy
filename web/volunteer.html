Here are the information about volunteerings

Alice Fong Yu Alternative School is an exemplary educational community in large part because of parent involvement. Parents volunteer in the classroom, on governing councils such as the School Site Council and Parents Association, and for school-wide special events. The commitment and leadership of parents at AFY provides a strong network of volunteers with a can-do attitude and who support the uniquely high standards of the AFY principal, teachers, and staff. Parents are encouraged to volunteer. Some weekly volunteer opportunities are as follows:

  • Class parent
  • Reading in Chinese or English
  • Morning drop-off zone
  • Before or after-school tutoring
  • Art class assistance
  • Garden assistance

AFY offers ample special events, which are not feasible without parent volunteers. These are rewarding, memory-making endeavors for parents and their AFY children.

  • China Cultural Exchange Program
  • Chinese New Year Parade
  • Fall Carnival
  • Science Fair (Middle School)
  • Spring Festival
  • Spring Auction
  • Walk the Great Wall
