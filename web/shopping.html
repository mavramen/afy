Here are how you can help us

You can support our school in many ways.

Your shopping earns for Alice Fong Yu thousands of dollars.Our students win!

eScrip
www.escrip.com

Renew your Safeway Club Card and register your credit and debit cards at eScrip where a % of your purchases from hundreds of national retailers will go back to the school in the form of a donation.



Office Depot
www.officedepot.com

Mention AFY (school number 70014127) at the register and a percentage of your purchase will go back to the school in the form of a gift card good for office and school supplies.



Amazon
afypa.org/amazon

Click the link above and save as a bookmark. The link above takes you to smile.amazon.com and includes the AFY id (afypa03-20). Smile.amazon.com will donate 0.5% (please choose Alice Fong Yu Parents Association as the charity) on top of the (up to) 15% of purchases that go to AFY.

Shoparoo
www.shoparoo.com

Require smartphone, ipad, tablet and Shoparoo App.

1. Shop for groceries anywhere, buy anything (any store, any products!)
2. Snap pictures of the receipts with the free app.
3. AFY earns money for each receipt picture taken!

Sports Basement
www.sportsbasement.com

Go to your favorite Sports Basement, sign up for the Basementeers program and choose Alice Fong Yu as your beneficiary. 10% off for you and 10% of profits back to the school.
