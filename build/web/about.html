About Immersion Education at Alice Fong Yu

Immersion education is an exciting and innovative program in which children develop the ability to speak, read, and write in a second language. Knowledge of more than one language and culture is important for our children’s full participation in a culturally and linguistically diverse world. The educational leaders of San Francisco Unified School District believe that the opportunity to become fluent in a second language should be available to all children. At Alice Fong Yu , the school curriculum (math, science, social studies, etc.) is taught primarily in Chinese during grades K-3, with an increase in English instructional time during 4th and 5th grades. By 5th grade, children can communicate effectively in Chinese and English and have fulfilled all requirements for promotion to Middle School. Over thirty years of research conducted in immersion schools throughout Canada and the United States has shown that immersion students score above average in tests of their academic skills, while achieving a remarkable degree of fluency in the second language.

Educational Approach

Underlying the classroom practices and curriculum choices at Alice Fong Yu is the belief that children’s social and emotional growth is as important as their academic development. The broad educational goals of the program are:

  • To instill a love of learning
  • To nurture self-esteem
  • To foster respect of others

All of the classrooms emphasize small group, hands-on, cooperative learning activities. Students develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills through student-directed projects. Such projects also promote language acquisition as students use their Chinese to discuss choices and procedures.

Growth and Learning Opportunities – GLO

Growth and Learning Opportunities – GLO- here to serve our mission of “empowering youth and families to be leaders toward equitable communities”. In meeting this mission, we are equipping youth with the tools necessary to blossom into respectable adults so that they may continue the foundation of leading and giving back to their communities. In striving to reach this goal, we provide after school programming at 5 elementary schools in San Francisco. We operate a fun and engaging summer camp open to all Bay Area elementary aged children. We provide enrichment opportunities that enhance and reinforce what our children are learning during the school day. We provide hands on training opportunities for our staff, many of whom are college students embarking on a career path to being a Credentialed Teacher, School Administrator and/or Youth Worker. We offer our families a safe place to learn and thrive.

Education Outside

Education Outside advances science education and environmental literacy by teaching outdoors in public schools. We spark children's interest in science and connect them to the natural world through hands-on learning in school gardens..
