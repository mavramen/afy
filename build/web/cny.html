Chinese New Year Benefit Banquet fundraiser /

Here is the information about CNY Banquet

The annual CNY Benefit Banquet celebrates our outstanding Cultural Exchange Program (CEP) and China Study Trip (Beijing, Xi'an, Hong Kong!). This fundraising event is hosted by the 8th grade students and their families and emceed by AFY staff chaperones.

You'll be entertained by lion dancers, speeches and skits, and much more! Join us to ring in the New Year!

In addition, CNY Raffle tickets are going home in the Wednesday folder. Buy a ticket ($1/per ticket or $10 for 12) for a chance to win:
 
  • Grand Prize: 7 Day Cruise for 2 from Cruises & Beyond ($1500 value)
  • First Prize: $888 cash
  • Second Prize: iPad Pro ($699 value)
  • Third Prize: Apple Watch Series 3 ($329 value)
  • and 20 themed gift baskets (value $90 and up)!
Incentive prizes for top elementary and middle school ticket sellers. You need not be present at the banquet to win! All proceeds go to the AFYPA General Fund which supports the CEP!
