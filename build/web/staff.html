GLO - Growth and Learning Opportunities

Growth and Learning Opportunities – GLO- here to serve our mission of “empowering youth and families to be leaders toward equitable communities”. In meeting this mission, we are equipping youth with the tools necessary to blossom into respectable adults so that they may continue the foundation of leading and giving back to their communities. In striving to reach this goal, we provide after school programming at 5 elementary schools in San Francisco. We operate a fun and engaging summer camp open to all Bay Area elementary aged children. We provide enrichment opportunities that enhance and reinforce what our children are learning during the school day. We provide hands on training opportunities for our staff, many of whom are college students embarking on a career path to being a Credentialed Teacher, School Administrator and/or Youth Worker. We offer our families a safe place to learn and thrive.

Education Outside

Education Outside advances science education and environmental literacy by teaching outdoors in public schools. We spark children's interest in science and connect them to the natural world through hands-on learning in school gardens..
